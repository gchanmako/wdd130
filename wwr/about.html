The White Water Rafting Company

History

THE Whitewater Rafting Company is the premier outfitter of whitewater enthusiasts. Founded in 1985 we have followed our passion for shooting the finest rapids and bringing that experience to you.

We pride ourselves in offering safe yet challenging rafting adventures with our experienced guides. Our unique adventure packages are for all ages and skill levels and make for an affordable diversion from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Based on the Salt River in Arizona our vast experience and exploration of the region shows in our perfectly guided expeditions. So come take a trip with us and shoot the rapids down the Salt River. It will be an adventure you will not forget.
